     1вҶ’     1вҶ’     1вҶ’Project Vision: A Citizen-Centric Omnichannel Ecosystem
     2вҶ’     2вҶ’     2вҶ’
     3вҶ’     3вҶ’     3вҶ’I am conceptualizing a centralized, omnichannel Point-of-Sale (PoS) and e-commerce ecosystem designed to unite merchants, service providers, and consumers on a single, unified platform. Operating on a Hybrid PaaS backend with a suite of user-friendly SaaS frontends, this platform functions much like a Google account: users log in once to access a vast, interconnected network of distinct services and products.
     4вҶ’     4вҶ’     4вҶ’
     5вҶ’     5вҶ’     5вҶ’Core Innovation: "Single Identity, Multiple Roles" Our primary goal is to build a unified, citizen-centric digital public infrastructure that leverages Bangladesh's mature Mobile Financial Services (MFS) ecosystem. The standout feature is our "Single Identity, Multiple Roles" model. Unlike fragmented apps that require separate accounts, a user maintains one verified identity and one unified wallet. With this single account, a citizen can seamlessly switch between SaaS interfaces to buy groceries in the morning (Consumer), sell farm produce in the afternoon (Merchant), and accept plumbing jobs in the evening (Service Provider).
